FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?
+

The timeline depends on the complexity of the project. A basic website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects can take 4-8 weeks or longer.

What is your pricing structure?
+

We offer three main packages: Basic ($300), Standard ($600), and Premium ($1,200). Each package includes different features and levels of service. We also provide custom quotes for more complex projects.

Do you provide ongoing support?
+

Yes, we offer website maintenance packages to keep your site updated, secure, and performing well after launch. Our Premium package includes 3 months of support.

Can you help with website content?
+

While we primarily focus on design and development, we can provide guidance on content creation and connect you with trusted content specialists if needed.
